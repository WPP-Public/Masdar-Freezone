Join Abu Dhabi’s Fastest-Growing Life Sciences and Biotechnology Cluster at Masdar City Free Zone

The Masdar City Free Zone Life Sciences Cluster in Abu Dhabi is a rapidly expanding hub for life sciences and biotechnology, supported by the region’s most advanced R&D institutions, leading CROs, and government-backed initiatives from the Department of Health Abu Dhabi (DoH) and the Abu Dhabi Investment Office. This cluster provides unparalleled opportunities for collaboration, innovation, and growth within a strategically located free zone in Abu Dhabi, making it an ideal choice for businesses involved in life sciences and BioTech in Abu Dhabi, and beyond.

Custom Facilities, Investment, and World-Class
Infrastructure for Life Sciences in Abu Dhabi

With total investments exceeding AED 1 billion in the Life Sciences cluster, Masdar City Free Zone is set to become a leading hub for life sciences in Abu Dhabi. One of the unique aspects of the Masdar City Free Zone is our ability to provide build-to-suit facilities, catering specifically to the high specifications and requirements of biotech companies. This flexibility allows for the creation of custom spaces that support advanced research and development needs.

The life sciences cluster in Masdar City Free Zone includes significant resources like the Middle East's Bio-Bank and the region’s first commercial-scale vivarium supporting drug discovery. It also benefits from a dense network of research institutions, hospitals, incubators, and accelerators, fostering collaboration between academia, industry, and healthcare providers. This vibrant ecosystem drives innovation and accelerates the commercialization of scientific discoveries.

Hosting over 50 biotechnology companies, the Free Zone boasts some of the most advanced R&D facilities in the region, promoting cutting-edge research in rare diseases, genomics, cell and gene therapy, and both pre-clinical and clinical trials, making Masdar City Free Zone a premier destination for innovation in Life Sciences in Abu Dhabi.

Government Support and Policy: Fostering
Innovation in Life Sciences

Abu Dhabi’s government is deeply committed to advancing the life sciences sector, as demonstrated by its robust policies, incentives, and initiatives. This commitment strengthens Abu Dhabi’s position as a leading hub for Life Sciences and BioTech. Key initiatives and leading bodies include: 

emirati genome program

The groundbreaking national initiative aimed at mapping the genetic makeup of Emirati citizens using advanced DNA sequencing and artificial intelligence. By generating comprehensive genomic data, the program seeks to enhance personalized and preventive healthcare for the UAE population, offering deeper insights into rare genetic disorders and enabling the development of new, targeted treatments.

The Malafi Initiative

Abu Dhabi’s Health Information Exchange platform, which integrates patient records across healthcare facilities, enabling more efficient and effective clinical decision-making. By providing seamless access to comprehensive patient data, Malafi enhances coordination among healthcare providers and improves patient outcomes.
The continued alignment between Masdar City (MC) and cluster regulators ensures a streamlined regulatory environment that fosters innovation and growth within the life sciences cluster. Masdar City's strategic location also provides direct access to key regulatory bodies, such as the Emirates Drug Establishment, Public Health Centre, and the Department of Health. Moreover, businesses benefit from direct access to the healthcare network and opportunities for clinical trials within Abu Dhabi hospitals. This integrated approach helps Masdar City Free Zone create a supportive environment that encourages the growth and success of life sciences companies in Abu Dhabi.
